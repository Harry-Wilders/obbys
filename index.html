About Us

Who We are

Obby’s Ventures Innovating Your Event Experience

Obby’s Ventures Ltd is a renowned provider of technical, consulting, planning, design, and implementation services for professional events. Based in Nairobi, we operate throughout the region, helping clients bring their event visions to life.

Event PlanningBring your vision to life

We offer a range of specialized planning, tracking, and reporting tools to help you bring your event to fruition. Our proprietary software provides a detailed roadmap for each event while also serving as a blueprint for future events. Throughout the planning process, we prioritize your objectives to ensure that all deliverables are met. Whether it's a corporate conference, a wedding, or a community event, we've got you covered.

Technical Production Boost your coverage

We pride ourselves on our cutting-edge event technology. Our technical production services include audio-visual services, filming, live streaming, broadcast services, recording, editing, and archiving of digital content created during your event.

Ground Transportation ServicesComfort travel and luxury

We provide ground transportation services and logistics for your travel needs with a focus on luxury and comfort. Our top-of-the-line brands ensure that you can travel with ease and without any worries. We take care of planning the trip, assigning guides, and taking you to the destinations of your choice.
