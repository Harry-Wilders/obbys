About Us

Obby’s Ventures Limited is an integrated technical, consulting, planning, design, and implementation provider for professional events based in Nairobi operating in the region.

Our speciality and expertise

We specialize in rentals of Audio-Visual technology including:

  1. Sound
  2. Screens
  3. Lighting
  4. Digital and Interactive screens

Our expertise combined with our extensive inventory of AV technologies gives us the unique ability to serve our clients, in a variety of industries, from concept to completion.

Our Goal

Obby’s Ventures provides high-quality, innovative solutions for professional events using the latest audio-visual technology. We aim to exceed client expectations, enhance brand image and audience engagement, and continually improve to lead in the industry.

  • To provide high-quality and reliable AV solutions for various types of events, such as conferences, exhibitions, concerts, and weddings.
  • To expand our market share and reach new customers in the region, especially in the emerging sectors of education, health, and tourism.
  • To innovate and adopt new technologies and trends in the AV industry, such as virtual reality, augmented reality, and holograms.
  • To enhance our brand reputation and customer satisfaction by delivering exceptional service and support.
  • To foster a culture of excellence, creativity, and teamwork among our staff and partners.
